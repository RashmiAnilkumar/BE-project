About the Project

   The information from social networking sites could be used in many ways, one such purpose is sentimental analysis. The thoughts and communication skills on the social media websites like Twitter, Facebook and Instagram is reflecting and impacting the society. Taking such tweets from twitter and using such tweets to classify it into polarized ones would be beneficial in business and even other domains.

    Sentiment is an opinion or thought that is expressed, it is about what one feels about something. The process of classifying such opinions or reviews which is expressed through text to determine the attitude towards a particular topic if it is positive, negative or neutral is called sentiment analysis. Sentiment is used to categorize and highlight the degree of positive response, negative response or a neutral.

    The project aims to build models that analyze people's opinion on the statue of unity using twitter dataset. Statue of Unity is a colossal statue of an Indian Independence activist Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel. The result of the project will be a model that classifies the impression of people on the topic the world's tallest statue, statue of unity if is positive, negative or neutral.
